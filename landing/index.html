How Kabootar works

Three steps whether you're a traveler or a sender.

TRAVELER
🧳

Post your trip

Going from Delhi to Mumbai? Post your route, date, and available weight. Set your price per kg.

SENDER
📦

Post a request

Need to send documents or electronics? Post what, where, and how much it weighs. We find matches.

BOTH
💬

Connect & deliver

Chat directly, agree on price, hand off the parcel. Rate each other after delivery.

Who It's For

Built for both sides

✈️ For Travelers

Turn your trip into income

You're already traveling. Carry a parcel and earn extra without any extra effort.

  • Post trips in under 60 seconds
  • You set your own price per kg
  • Only carry what you're comfortable with
  • Build your trust rating over time
📦 For Senders

Send anything, anywhere, cheaper

Stop overpaying couriers. Send your parcel with a real person going the same route.

  • Find matches on your exact route
  • See traveler ratings before connecting
  • Chat directly — no middlemen
  • Great for documents, clothes, electronics
