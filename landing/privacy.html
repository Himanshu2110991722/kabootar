Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Applies to the Kabutar app and website  ·  Governing law: India (IT Act 2000)

Kabutar is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We never sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Kabutar ("we", "us", "our") operates the Kabutar mobile application and website at kabutar.in. For privacy-related questions, contact us at kabutar.support@gmail.com.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the service — Display your profile, match you with travelers/senders, enable chat.
  • Verify identity — Phone OTP and KYC document review to prevent fraud.
  • Trust and safety — Ratings, reviews, and delivery photo audit trails.
  • Communication — Send OTP codes, important service notifications (not marketing spam).
  • Platform improvement — Understand how the app is used to improve features.
  • Legal compliance — Respond to lawful requests from Indian authorities.

4. Location Data

Location is requested only when you tap "Near Me" to filter nearby trips and parcels. We do not track your location in the background. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of your session. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

6. KYC Documents

KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving Licence) and selfies are uploaded to Firebase Storage and reviewed only by authorized Kabutar administrators. Documents are used solely for identity verification and are not shared with other users or third parties except as required by Indian law. You may request deletion of your KYC documents by emailing kabutar.support@gmail.com.

7. Chat Messages

Chat messages between users are stored on our servers to enable conversation history. Messages are not read by Kabutar staff unless required for dispute resolution, fraud investigation, or compliance with a valid legal order. We do not sell or share chat content with third parties.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Between users — Your name, profile photo, rating, hometown, and masked phone number are visible to other users on the platform.
  • Law enforcement — When required by a valid court order or legal process under Indian law.
  • Safety — To prevent imminent harm or report illegal activity.
  • Service providers — Only the third parties listed in Section 5, strictly for operating the platform.

9. Phone Number Masking

Your full phone number is never shown to other users. We display a masked version (e.g., +91XXXXX43210) on public profiles and trip/parcel cards. Your full number is only visible to you in your own profile settings.

10. Data Retention

  • Active accounts — Data retained as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts — Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Chat messages — Retained for 12 months for dispute resolution, then deleted.
  • KYC documents — Retained for 3 years as required by Indian KYC regulations, then deleted.
  • Delivery photos — Retained for 6 months, then deleted.

11. Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act 2000 and related rules, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction — Update inaccurate data through the app settings.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdrawal — Revoke consent for optional data (e.g., location) at any time.

To exercise these rights, email kabutar.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Children's Privacy

Kabutar is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover an account belonging to a minor, it will be immediately deleted.

13. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Security Rules for file storage access control
  • JWT tokens for API authentication (30-day expiry)
  • Rate limiting on all authentication endpoints
  • MongoDB Atlas encryption at rest

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to kabutar.support@gmail.com.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
